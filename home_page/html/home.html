Training and Placement Cell

About:

The primary objective of the placement cell is to provide job opportunities to the students by organizing recruitment drives, job fairs, and networking events. The cell also works towards the overall development of the students by providing them with the required training and guidance. The placement cell establishes a strong relationship with the industry, inviting companies to the campus for recruitment, arranging internships for the students, and conducting workshops on industry-related topics. The role of the placement cell is crucial in bridging the gap between the academic and corporate world and ensuring a smooth transition for the students from campus to corporate life.
